About Us

English Genie is an online organisation which offers excellent proficiency courses in the English language. We are experts in the field of English as a second language acquisition. These courses are designed by experienced course developers in the field. Our teaching methods and materials have contemporary societal value.

The teaching is WhatsApp based which helps in keeping communication open and consistent. We provide personalized support 24/7 to our learners. We provide the best platform to experience the power of social learning, and get inspired by an national network of learners , share ideas with your peers and course educators on every step of the course.

Our courses have been tested and evaluated in other regions. Currently, expanding it to the Telugu region for the betterment in language learning to keep the people on par with current trends and needs of the society on a global level. English Genie has helped many candidates achieve their goals, especially language and skills specific.
