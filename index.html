Welcome To

English Genie

English Genie is an online organisation which offers excellent proficiency courses in the English language. We are experts in the field of English as a second language acquisition. These courses are designed by experienced course developers in the field. Our teaching methods and materials have contemporary societal value.

The teaching is WhatsApp based which helps in keeping communication open and consistent. We provide personalized support 24/7 to our learners. We provide the best platform to experience the power of social learning, and get inspired by an national network of learners , share ideas with your peers and course educators on every step of the course.

Our Courses

01

English Genie offers an extensive course on English language skills LSRWVG (Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing, Vocabulary and Grammar). We offer courses for beginners, intermediate and advanced level candidates.When it comes to language learning, the holistic development of the learner depends on the mastery of all the skills and sub-skills of that particular language, especially when it's English. The individual units of the language course deal with the two important areas of vocabulary and grammar.

02

We offer an English course for the duration of TWO months which includes classes through audios and worksheets for practice. We provide error correction classes and remedial materials alongside. Our courses are segregated into weeks, activities, and steps to help you keep track of your learning. Learn through a mix of bite-sized audios, long- and short-form articles, reading passages and practical activities. Our courses keep the learners motivated by using the progress page to keep track of their step completion and assessment scores.
